# Review of Clumping factor and Power Spectrum in AIDA-TNG simulations

This review collects the locally generated AIDA-TNG plots. The model-evolution
panels use overdensity on the horizontal axis and show one curve per available
snapshot; the snapshot labels identify the redshift sequence.

### Model comparison

The corresponding absolute per-snapshot power-spectrum comparisons are stored
next to the ratio panels under the same `model-comparison` tree. The generated
products cover snapshots 017, 025, 033, 050, 067, 084, and 099 for both
simulation sizes and for gas and dark matter.
